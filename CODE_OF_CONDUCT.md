# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

All participants in the nokey community are expected to be respectful and constructive. Read the full text at the link above.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **security@nokey.ai**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
